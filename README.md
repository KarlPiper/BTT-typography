# BTT-typography
 BetterTouchTool actions for typing special characters.

### Punctuation

Char.|Shortcut|Name
-----|--------|----
– | ⌥- | Endash
— | ⌥⇧- | Emdash
• | ⌥8 | Bullet
· | ⌥⇧9 | Interpunct
… | ⌥; | Ellipsis
“ | ⌥\[ | L Double Quote
” | ⌥⇧\[ | R Double Quote
‘ | ⌥] | L Single Quote
’ | ⌥⇧] | R Single Quote
